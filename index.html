LibraTrack Journey

"A room without books is like a body without a soul."

— Marcus Tullius Cicero

LibraTrack is designed to help schools manage operations efficiently. It replaces manual record-keeping with a digital system, reducing errors and saving time for librarians and students. The system allows easy cataloging of books, quick searches by title, author, or category, and simple tracking of borrowing and returns. Librarians can monitor overdue and low-stock books, while students enjoy a user-friendly platform to explore, borrow, and manage their reading history.
